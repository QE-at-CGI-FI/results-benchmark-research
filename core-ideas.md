**Skill** is the ability to perform a task so that it produces results it should, quantitatively and qualitatively.

**Experience** is gained by performing a task, without necessarily receiving feedback on the results.

**Programmatic testing** is use of programming for purposes of testing software. We use this term to distinguish from ideas of manual and automated testing, where the two are treated as interconnected activities where coverage of results makes sense to compare.

**Manual testing** is a style of testing that centers in writing instructions on what and how to test, test cases, and executing them by hand.

**Automated testing** is a style of testing that centers in writing instructions on what and how to test, test cases, and executing them with the help of tools.

**Exploratory testing** is a style of testing that centers in learning about the software by interacting with it, and using the learning to guide further interactions.
